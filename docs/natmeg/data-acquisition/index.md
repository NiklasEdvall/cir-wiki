---
title: Data Acquisition at NatMEG
---

At NatMEG, MEG (± EEG) data is done in pairs.

Person A: Responsible for participant preparation, interaction and running experiment.
Person B: Technical preparation and MEG recording monitoring.

General workflow:

| When                              | Person A                          | Person B                          | 
|-----------------------------------|-----------------------------------|-----------------------------------|
| Before ppt arrival                | Check experiment and equipment    | Turn on and OPM system            |
| PPT in prep area                  | Prepare ppt                       | Record empty room                 |
| PPT in MSR                        | Adjust sensors                    | Adjust sensors                    |
| Before recording                  | Test experiment                   | Initiate and localize sensors     |
| During recording                  | Run experiment, communicate       | Record and monitor                |
| After recording                   | Bring PPT out                     | Record empty room                 |
| After recording                   | Clean up                          | Run SESHAT                        |
